My Grade 10 Technology Experience

This class was hands down my favorite! I had the chance to reconnect with old friends, and Mr. Hodgson, our teacher, was amazing. He made every lesson so interesting, and I learned so many new things, from coding to website design and everything in between. It was a great year, and I’m so grateful for this experience.

What I Wish I Could Have Learned More About:

If I had more time, I would love to explore more advanced coding techniques, as well as diving deeper into computer hardware. But overall, it was an incredible class that sparked my interest in technology!
