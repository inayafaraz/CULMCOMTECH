Binary Conversion Guide

Welcome to the Binary Conversion Guide! Here you'll learn how to convert binary to decimal and decimal to binary.

1. Convert Binary to Decimal Numbers

Follow these steps to convert a binary number to its decimal equivalent:

Step 1

Write down the binary number.

Step 2

Start from the right, multiply each digit by 2 raised to the power of its position, starting at 0.

Step 3

Add up all the results to get the decimal number.

2. Convert Decimal to Binary Numbers

Follow these steps to convert a decimal number to its binary equivalent:

Step 1

Write down the decimal number.

Step 2

Divide the number by 2 and write down the remainder.

Step 3

Repeat the division by 2 until the quotient is 0. Write down the remainder in reverse order to get the binary number.
